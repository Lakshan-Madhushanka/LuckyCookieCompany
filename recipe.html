Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter or margarine, softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Baking Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 375°F.
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Beat butter, sugar, and brown sugar until creamy.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla, then mix well.
  5. Gradually add dry ingredients.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking tray.
  8. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes.
